The Rise of Artificial Intelligence

In the 21st century artificial intelligence has swiftly developed from the realm of fantasy to become one of the most dominating fields in the technological world. Presently AI can be found integrated into the operation of every aspect of life; this is from common applications like the voice assistant in your phone, or a recommendation system used on social media to the highly complex and intelligent medical diagnostics devices, even to the level of autonomous cars driving down a street.

A prominent factor of the surge in artificial intelligence technology development is the vast amount of information available to these systems. Each second of everyday life more data is generated by the existing technology at an unprecedented speed than ever before. Using Machine learning (a sub-field of AI) it is now possible for machines to recognize patterns and make conclusions using little to no human input.

Currently artificial intelligence is being introduced to the health care profession with the prospect of discovering diseases quicker and with greater accuracy than before. AI systems have been developed that can analyse medical images and recognize any inconsistencies while also aiding doctors in life or death situations.

Artificial intelligence technology has had an enormous impact on the transportation world. Self driving cars that were once the stuff of pure science fiction, are now being built and tested. These cars require AI in order for them to successfully interpret the world around them and to make instantaneous decisions.

Even with the positives, ethical issues concerning AI are also developing. There is worry on whether jobs will be lost to machines as tasks become more automated and there are also concerns about data and who has control of the decision making. It is crucial as machines take over roles, that it is done in a fair and ethical manner.

There are various governments working with their own standards and policies to oversee AI development. The hope is to achieve the goals of innovation with accountability.

Overall Artificial intelligence is changing the world, and this advancement can bring great opportunities but it also has potential downfalls that society needs to prepare for. Whether AI continues to grow positively is up to both the advancements and responsible application of technology.
